# P2P Share

Ever been on a group trip in the mountains or in a different country without an active data plan, and struggled to share 4K videos or raw photos instantly with friends?

**P2P Share** leverages ad-hoc Wi-Fi routing and bluetooth protocols to allow hyper-fast transfer of media files locally, with **no internet required.**

## The Problem With Sharing Today
- AirDrop only works for Apple ecosystems.
- Cloud sharing (Drive/Photos) requires robust internet and compresses media unless specified otherwise.
- Chat apps crush image quality.

P2P Share builds a seamless local networking environment bridging iOS and Android instantly, letting you share memories the moment they happen.
