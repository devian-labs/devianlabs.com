# Khao - The Digital Menu Revolution

Small vendors deserve big tech, zero complexity, and no crazy overhead. 
**Khao** is our digital solution for small restaurants, street food vendors, and pop-up events to instantly turn their static menus into fully functional, scannable QR ordering systems.

## Why Khao?
- **Zero App Download**: Customers scan the QR, order from their browser, and pay.
- **Easy Menu Updates**: Sold out of an item? Disable it with a toggle.
- **Affordable**: Scaled perfectly for small businesses.

Empowering local food vendors with the tech parity of large chains.
